Planet Factoids

Stellar Insights!

After being inspired by my visit to the NASA JFK Center here in Florida, and to practice with React more, I decided to build this planet facts project. It includes:

  • Facts about all eight planets.
  • A starry background with three images for each planet.
  • A responsive design.

Due to other commitments, I had to rush the mobile design for the site, but it is still functional. Eventually I hope to come back and refine the design.
